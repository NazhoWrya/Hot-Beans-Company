Job Specifications

  • Front-End Development

    • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
    • Experience with front-end frameworks such as React, Vue.js, or Angular.

  • Back-End Development

    • Familiarity with server-side languages like Node.js, Python, or PHP.
    • Understanding of databases (SQL and NoSQL) and ORM frameworks.

    • Ability to work on both front-end and back-end technologies.
    • Knowledge of RESTful APIs and microservices architecture.

    • Experience with Git for version control and collaborative development.

  • Problem-Solving Skills

    • Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
    • Ability to debug and troubleshoot issues across different layers of the application.

  • UI/UX Design Awareness

    • Understanding of UI/UX design principles.
    • Ability to collaborate effectively with designers to implement visual and interactive elements.
